# AI Use Disclosure — Personal Layer Setup Exercise

**What the AI did:** Claude extracted `search/resume.json` from my resume (first from a `.tex` draft, then re-extracted from `Resume.pdf`), drafted `search/profile.yml` from my answers to the intake questions, and drafted `search/gaps.md` by comparing the attested resume against real job-posting and O*NET evidence it looked up.

**What the AI could not do:** When Claude drafted `profile.yml` from my intake answers, it recorded my target company stage as "growth-stage (Series A-C)." Separately, when it re-extracted `resume.json` from my actual resume PDF, the resume's own summary line said I was "looking to join an early-stage team." Claude had produced both documents and did not catch that they contradicted each other — it took my reading both files side by side to notice the AI artifact I'd validated (profile.yml) actually didn't match my real resume's stated target. I corrected profile.yml to "early-stage (seed / Series A)" and also flagged that this changes the sponsorship picture: early-stage companies sponsor visas less often than growth-stage ones, which Claude had not weighed when treating sponsorship as a context-independent hard gate. That tension is noted in `profile.yml` as open research, not resolved — the AI could draft the gate, it couldn't tell me the gate would bind harder against my actual target stage.
